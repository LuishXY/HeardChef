Heard Chef

Welcome to the platform for Food and Beverage professionals.

Hear Chef is an app that focuses on bringing talented people in the food and beverage industry together.

We provide the proper resources for people who want to become experts in the industry.

  • Get Started

Serve Everywhere

We are gaining connections to a diverse range of restaurants and providing on demand staffing. Allowing you to become a flexible asset in the whole restaurants scene. You will be allowed to take shifts and get compensated.

  • Learn More

Food Tastings

The Heard Chef platform will allow you to find local food tastings and restaurant menu studies. Our mission is to empower people throughout the whole industry and advance their knowledge about ingredients, flavors, and pairings all while trying delicious food.

  • Learn More

Wine Tastings

Expand you ready to expand your knowledge about wines and grape varietals. Our platform will allow you to find local Sommelier support/ study groups. We will provide the eco-system for wine enthusiasts, so you can network and expand your wine knowledge.
